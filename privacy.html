🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Data We Collect

Listahan collects minimal data to provide the service:

  • Username and password (stored in Google Sheets)
  • Your anime/manga/manhwa list entries (title, status, rating, notes, etc.)
  • Security question and answer (optional, for password recovery)

How We Store Your Data

All data is stored in a private Google Sheets spreadsheet owned by the developer. Your data is tied to your username and only accessible when you are logged in. We do not share your data with third parties.

Passwords

⚠️ Note: Passwords are currently stored as plain text in Google Sheets. Please do not reuse passwords from other important accounts. We recommend using a unique password for Listahan.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage to remember your login session and theme preference. No tracking cookies are used. You can clear your session at any time by clicking "Logout".

Your Rights

  • You can delete any entry from your list at any time
  • You can change your password using the forgot password feature
  • You can contact the developer to request full data deletion

Contact

For any privacy concerns, please visit the Contact page and reach out to the developer.

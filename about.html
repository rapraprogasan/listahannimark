About Listahan ng AnimeManga

Your personal tracker for Anime, Manga, and Manhwa — so you'll never forget a title again.

📺 What is Listahan?

Listahan (Filipino word for "list") is a personal tracker app where you can save all the anime, manga, and manhwa titles you've watched or plan to watch — with ratings, episode counts, notes, and more.

☁️ Cloud-Powered

All your data is stored in Google Sheets via Google Apps Script. Your list is accessible from any device as long as you log in. No data lost!

🔒 Private Accounts

Each user has their own private list. Create an account with a username and password — your data is completely separate from other users.

🎯 Features

  • ✅ Anime, Manga, Manhwa tracking
  • ⭐ Rating system (0–10)
  • 📝 Personal notes per title
  • 🎬 YouTube trailer embed
  • 🔗 Streaming site links
  • 🌙 Dark / Light mode
  • 🔍 Search your collection
  • 📊 Dashboard with stats
